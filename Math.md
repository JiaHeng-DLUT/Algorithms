## Multiplicative Inverse

- Find the multiplicative inverse of 5 (mod 72)

$$
\begin{align*}
5x&\equiv1(mod 72)\\
5x&=72y+1\\
-72y+5x&=1\\
-(14*5+2)y+5x&=1\\
5(x-14y)-2y&=1\\
(5-4&=1)\\
y&=2\\
x-14y&=1\\
x&=1+14y=1+14*2=29
\end{align*}
$$
